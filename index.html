About Your Required Meetings

The Church asks that you meet with your sponsor in person (or by video call) three times as you move through this course. These meetings are not a test — they are a gift. Your sponsor will listen, reflect with you, and pass your written reflections to Fr. O'Brien before your Rite of Acceptance.

What to bring to each meeting

  • Your written reflections from the sections you've completed
  • Any questions or doubts you've encountered — nothing is off-limits
  • An open heart and at least an hour of uninterrupted time
